Infrastructure of Wellington

Wellington, like any major city, is filled with infrastructure that not only enables the city to run both smoothly and coherently but also framework that helps the interent function. Whether it be power generators that facilitate power for servers or underground cables that transport data forwards and backwards there is a deep system - ofen unseen - that aids the workings of the city. The internet is a large invisible being and hence needs a great amount of infrastructure for it to work properly. Things such as parking meters and road also help in the order of the city in terms of general infrastructure as opposed to the internet.
